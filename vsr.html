ima:

Transportation & UX design
May.2017 - Jun.2017
Personal work

Intro:
Honda VSR is a vertical vehicle and a corresponding intelligent transportation sharing system for 2030 city commuter.

Design Challenge:
Autopilot technology will change the driving behavior and electric engine will change the cabin layout. With these changes, what would the city commuters' commuting experience be like in 2030.

Highlight:
The combination of road jogging and city commuting makes daily life healthier and more efficient. The self-driving vehicles are units of the sharing system, which will serve as a major part of the public transportation system and effectively solve the city tidal traffic problem.
