"What is the cube?" is a tiny cubical installation designed to reveal the Idealism.

Everyone has a different perception of the world for complex reasons. I set triggers in this installation to mimic the complexity and possibility of the world, player would get unique results on the screen because their unique pressing way (the first button to press, the speed of pressing buttons ,the speed of pressing a single button and the random values in the code). I hope the question " What is the cube?" can drive player to attempt to explain the installation and get to know the diversity of human opinions towards the same "simple" stuff. Everything seems logic but everything is also uncontrollable and random.

I am facing a coding problem that I can not have a Serial signal to execute boolean and I am still trying to figure out. The shell of the installation is under construction using cardboard and wooden stick. The buttons need to be welded to wires in order to be embedded into the small shell which is not large enough to hold the breadboard.
