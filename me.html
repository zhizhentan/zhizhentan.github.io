Hi there! I am Zhizhen (Jerry) Tan.

I am a NYC based UX designer, product designer, technology player, and crafter. I am currently a MFA Design & Technology candidate 2020' at Parsons the New School. Before that, I gained a BE degree of Indutrial Design from South China University of Technology.

I dedicate myself to designing and transforming the existing lifestyle and experiences using different techniques. During my graduate studies at Parsons, I devote a lot of energy to delving into machine learning technique, integrating it into my design process. and working on the future human- Machine relationship between people and machines. I am trying to interpret the possibility of human-machine symbiosis, which is a more natural combination of humans and technology in the future.
