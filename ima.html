ima:

UI & UX design
Oct.2017 - Nov.2017
Joint work with Yihong Liu, Shunchang Wang, Jianan Chen

Intro:
ima is an instant medical aid system with an Apps where you can both offer your qualified medical aid to other users in need and get medical aid from other qualified users.

Highlight:
Sudden physical discomfort is inevitable, much of which is not severe enough for you to call an ambulance for help, but is better for you to get certain medical aid.

From ima, you can get instant medical aid by the following 3 ways:

  1. Get corresponding medical aid from another qualified user around you
  2. Get information about which stores can procide corresponding medical aid
  3. Request a Uber-aid, a cooperative project between ima and Uber
