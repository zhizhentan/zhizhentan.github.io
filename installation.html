Instructions for strangers


Design commission
Design an installation for strangers to communicate with each other and interact with the surroundings in public areas.

Location
Bushwick Collective, 19 St Nicholas Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11237, USA. It is a outdoor gallery of graffiti artists from all over the world.

Research and observations
How do people interact with graffiti?

Pre engagement stage: Missionaries, whose ultimate mission is to bring smiles back to the cities, should walk in the cities with smiles. Some special apparels are designed for them to be relaxed, calm and look determined.

Mapping stage: Capture the smile data throughout the cities.
Exploring stage: Eye blocking device is also a screen. Missionaries refer to the smile mappings and go to the "Mecca" of the happiness in the cities.
Persuading stage: The display screen on the garment shows the positions of the detected passers-by using squares with "smiling"/"not smiling". It is a persuasive way to encourage them to smile.

Analyzing stage: Smile data is transformed to smile mappings, which are stored for further research.
